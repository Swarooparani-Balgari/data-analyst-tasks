##  Food delivery Analysis

## Objective
A food delivery platform wants to analyze orders to understand delivery performance, discount effectiveness, and restaurant revenue.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- SQL
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI

## Dataset
The dataset contains food delivery information including:
- OrderID
- OrderDate
- City
- Restaurant
- OrderValue
- DeliveryTime_min
- Discount
- PaymentMethod
- CustomerRating

## Analysis Performed
- Calculated total revenue
- Analysed revenue by restaurant
- Compared city-wise revenue trend
- Evaluated daily revenue trend
- Compared average delivery time by city

## Key Insights
- Delhi has generated the highest revenue across all other cities
- Spice Hub restaurant had the highest customer rating
- Spice Hub restaurant has dominated the all other restaurant's revenue
- Mumbai had the lowest average delivery time across all cities
- UPI Payment Method is the most common used method for payments
- On the 9th April 2026, had the highest revenue amongst all other days
