# Task 01 - Rides Analysis

## Objective
A ride sharing company wants to analyze trip data to understand peak demand, driver performance, and revenue trends.

## Tools Used
- Excel
- SQL
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI

## Dataset
The dataset contains
- TripID
- TripDate
- City
- VehicleType
- Distance_km
- FarePerKm
- SurgeMultiplier
- DriverRating
- PaymentMode

## Analysis Performed
- Calculated total revenue
- Analysed total revenue by vehicleType
- Compared city-wise revenue
- Evaluated Driver performance
- Compared daily revenue

## Key Insights
- Delhi has the highest revenue across all cities
- UPI PaymentMode has dominated the other payment modes
- VehicleType Mini has the highest driver rating compared to other vehicle types
- The TripID 8007 had the highest SurgeMultiplier and also recorded the highest driver rating
- On the 4th April 2026 had the highest revenue
